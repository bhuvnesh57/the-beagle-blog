The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior traking instincts, the beagle is the primary breed used as a detectiongod for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The Beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet according to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.
